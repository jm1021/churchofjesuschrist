Understanding Grace

Why is it so important to gain an understanding about grace? David Lang forwarded me and article.

  • Atonement. Appreciate and take full advantage of the atonement of Jesus Christ. Become Sons and Daughters of God.
  • Covenants. Gain enabling relationships with God the Father and His Son. Because of Jesus’ atonement, a grace (charis) relationship enables us to become like Heavenly Father. Become joint-errors with Jesus.
  • Faith, Reciprocal Relationship. Gain more spiritual gifts and blessings as we receive the gifts God has already given. Anciently, all gifts are reciprocal, covenantal and establish relationships.
